About

Alchemy

I offer creative services specializing in experience design and branding. My formal education is in graphic design and I apply those principles actively to the Web. I write semantic HTML and Modular CSS. I am also a fervent advocate of grid systems and a perpetual student of Bauhaus, De Stijl, and International Typographic Style.

Why alchemy? Alchemy was the predecessor to modern chemistry. Part philosophy, part art, part science. In that respect, it shares many similarities with design.

Alchemy in Design is about creating something extraordinary out of something ordinary, catalyzing your brand into an experience.

Manifesto

“Good design should be accessible to all and reach for the highest possible standard. It should be equally conscious of form and function without sacrificing one for the other. Most of all, it should solve a problem.”

I apply this manifesto to all of my works. Know with confidence that I accept every project with the intentions of making it my best work. Fees and pricing are merely a formality. I care for your brand, identity and image as if they were my own. By calling myself a professional, this is how it should be.
